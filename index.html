In-Depth Mechanics

Island Creation & Types

When you first run /is create, you will be presented with a GUI to select your starting island type. Unlike traditional servers, these types provide unique gameplay bonuses.

Island Type Environment Inherent Bonus (Base)
Normal Plains / Forest Balanced baseline. +5% overall island XP gain.
Jungle Overgrown / Vines +10% Crop growth speed. Increased passive mob spawns.
Snowy Ice / Snow Water freezes faster. Unique ice-based resource generation.
Desert Sand / Cacti +15% Mob drop rates. Increased hostile mob spawn rates.
Island Traits System

Upon creation, your island is assigned a unique trait. These traits fundamentally alter your progression strategy. You can view your trait with /is traits.

  • Farming Boost: Multiplies crop yields during harvests.
  • Lucky Drops: Increases the chance of rare blocks generating in cobble generators.
  • Aggressive Mobs: Mobs deal more damage but drop significantly better loot.
  • Defensive Buffs: Players take less damage while within island borders.

Late Game: Players can reroll their trait using /is reroll, which requires a specific rare item or currency.

Player Commands Reference (/is)

The master command for players is /is. Below is the full directory of arguments.

Core & Management Commands
CommandDescription
/is createOpens the GUI to choose your island type and create it.
/is homeTeleports you to your island's main spawn point.
/is infoDisplays island level, active trait, and current members.
/is settingsOpens the island settings GUI (toggles, permissions).
/is publicToggles whether other players can visit your island.
/is deleteDeletes your island permanently (Requires confirmation).
Social & Member Commands
CommandDescription
/is invite <player>Sends a co-op invite to a player.
/is accept / denyAccepts or denies a pending island invite.
/is kick <player>Removes a member from your island.
/is leaveLeaves your current co-op island.
/is promote <player>Upgrades a member's role (e.g., Member -> Mod).
/is demote <player>Downgrades a member's role.
Progression, Events & Stats
CommandDescription
/is skillsOpens the Island Skill Tree GUI.

/is upgradeShortcut command to purchase upgrades.
/is eventsShows currently active dynamic events on your island.
/is eventinfoProvides details on event mechanics and upcoming timers.
/is limitsViews your current build, block, and entity limits.
/is statsDisplays comprehensive island statistics.
Review & Leaderboard Commands
CommandDescription
/is visit <player>Teleports to a public island.
/is rate <1-5>Leaves a star rating for the visited island.
/is review <text>Writes a written review for the island.
/is reviewsViews all reviews left for an island.
/is like <id>Upvotes a specific helpful review.
/is topDisplays the leaderboard for highest-level islands.
/is featuredTeleports to developer/staff featured islands.

Staff & Admin Commands (/isadmin)

Moderation & Data Control
CommandDescription
/isadmin create <player>Force-creates an island for a specific player.
/isadmin delete <player>Force-deletes a player's island.
/isadmin reset <player>Resets an island back to default state.
/isadmin tp <player>Bypasses locks to teleport to any island.
/isadmin setlevel <p> <lvl>Sets the specific level of an island.
/isadmin addxp <p> <amt>Grants raw XP to an island's progression.
/isadmin settrait <p> <trait>Force-assigns a specific trait to an island.
/isadmin removereview <id>Deletes an inappropriate review.
/isadmin clearreviews <p>Wipes all reviews for a specific island.
/isadmin reloadReloads the SB-DatabaseCore config files.
/isadmin fixdata <player>Attempts to repair corrupted island database entries.
